Are You NFPA 70B Compliant?

Stay ahead of OSHA regulations and avoid costly fines with predictive maintenance.

Key NFPA 70B Updates:

  • Annual Thermographic Inspections: "Thermographic inspections shall be performed annually on electrical equipment to identify overheating components" - NFPA 70B.
  • Scheduled Maintenance: "Equipment found to have anomalies shall be scheduled for corrective maintenance in accordance with the severity of the issue" - NFPA 70B.
  • Predictive Maintenance: "Predictive maintenance, including infrared thermography, is essential to ensuring the reliability of electrical systems" - NFPA 70B.
